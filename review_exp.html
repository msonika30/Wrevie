Review based enhanced user experience

Stay at the crest of innovation by processing millions of textual customer reviews and creating a novel search system that relies on customer side data, which is trusted 12x times more than manufacturer side data. Improve user engagement, increase retention and boost your sales through feature-wise product discovery and enhanced search.

API for feature-wise customer rating

Wrevie’s API solution integrates with your customer reviews system to extract ratings for customizable categories and attributes of products and services. For the first time ever, we are integrating information regarding user credibility and authenticity of reviews into user-decision making process to serve their needs better. Integrate these feature-wise customer ratings directly into your website in tailored formats or use our API and customise results as per your requirements.

Enhance site search by incorporating customer review data

Build a powerful search engine that uses the attribute wise feature rating from our API to provide an intuitive, state of the art search engine for customers. This will enable customers to search for products and services satisfying specific needs such as ‘laptop with battery performance rating of 5’. With Wrevie, you can now obviate your users from the burden of reading through numerous reviews when searching for a specific attribute in the product, thus helping them make faster decisions, improving overall conversion and driving up sales.

Track and control merchant’s performance

Wrevie segregates the sentiments related to sellers from product reviews and integrates it at the merchant level to allow you to track listed merchants on various service level parameters. This will enable users to optimize purchase decisions based on merchant ratings along delivery times, authenticity of product and other merchant-characteristics. Also, keep your merchants informed in real-time on their best and worst performing features and help them improve their services.
