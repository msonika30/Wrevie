Position 1

UI/UX Kickass Expert

For building the product, Wrevie is looking for rockstars who live and breathe design. In addition to understanding user behaviour and the importance of a good user experience, the UI/UX associate will work closely with the core-team and will be responsible for leading efforts towards building the most awesome interface for clients and users. While experience does count, we value people’s passion more than anything else!

  • Prior experience with UI/ UX design

  • Familiarity with Photoshop, Sketch or any similar design softwares
  • Proficiency in UX design principles, concepts, paradigms and practices and hands-on UI development experience is a must. As mentioned earlier, for passionate people willing to learn on the job themselves, the must criteria can be warded off!
  • Ability to think from a user’s perspective. Ready to take on the challenge of building the most appealing product for the user.
  • Great design sense in presenting information in an interactive way
  • Experience with R/Python/Animation is a plus

Position 2

Website Developer/Designer

We are looking for experienced part-time or full-time developers/designers to work closely with project managers, stakeholders and design team members to develop specifications and make recommendations on the use of new and emerging technologies. Candidates should have a strong understanding of web technologies and willing to help design, implement, and launch major client-facing features and responsive API

  • 2+ years experience building complex front-end applications in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS.
  • 2+ years experience in web scraping
  • Familiarity with one or more lightweight frameworks like: Angular JS, Ember JS, MVC, Bootstrap
  • Strong sense for design and excellent understanding of usability for web and mobile
  • Experience with Databases: SQL, MySQL, Oracle and AWS, github is a plus.
  • Experience with Python is a plus
