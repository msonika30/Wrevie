Solutions

Closing the loop with customer feedback.

Wrevie’s novel technology can process textual customer reviews to provide attribute wise rating profile for products and services. Wrevie’s technology is the next innovation needed for businesses ranging from discovery platforms to e-commerce sites as well as for individual service providers and sellers such as hotels, restaurants, hostels and hospitals. In order to cater to the specific requirements of different clients, our solutions are customised for different types of businesses.

Review Management and Customer Response system

Reinvent your relationship with customers with Wrevie’s 360 degree real-time feedback for your business from across the internet. Now use your customer's voice as insights to formulate business strategies.

Review based enhanced user experience

Improve customer experience and retention through a novel review based search and product discovery platform
