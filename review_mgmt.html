Review Management and Customer Response system

Wrevie brings together 360 degree real-time feedback for your business along different relevant attributes in your business from across the internet, with the ability to respond to customer grievances and improve overall service quality for your customers. With Wrevie’s Review Management tool, we help you to reinvent your relationship with your customers.

Analytics tool for your customer reviews

Our tool collects customer reviews from all your online listing sites and analyses them to infer your business performance measures. The tool provides dynamic real-time feedback for your business in terms of your chosen attributes and helps you chalk out an action plan to alleviate customer grievances.

Real-time customer response

Our additional feature automatically marks customer reviews as ‘grievances’ based on their tone, identifies the area of grievance (eg: In case of a restaurant, food related/ambience related/service related etc) and provides the option of auto-response to such reviews posted in online pages, directly through the tool. With Wrevie, you can choose to respond to customers yourself or let our experts give your customers what they want. We help you understand your customer needs and efficiently navigate your services to maximise customer retention.

Be the first to hear your customers

Whether you build that new adventure zone at your hotel or launch that new dish at your restaurant, get to know in real-time how your customer engagement and different business attributes are being impacted by your investments. Wrevie provides your business the ability to assess the positive and negative impacts of specific changes to your services and products like never before!

See where you stand among your industry peers

We are all eager to learn from the world, but fail to connect with the limitless sea of stories because ‘it’s different for them’. Learning, in its current form is difficult because we cannot identify the world that matters to us. As a food truck owner in India, you may have little to learn from that popular high end restaurant operating in San Francisco. We all need context to learn and Wrevie provides you the ability to choose the sector, scale and geography of businesses that you intend to learn from or are interested in measuring your organization’s performance against.
