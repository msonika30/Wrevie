Welcome to Wrevie Demo!

Here we demonstrate the building block of Wrevie’s solutions:

  • We obtain real customer reviews and the corresponding attribute wise ratings from users based on their experience.
  • The attributes are customizable by the user. Here, we choose attributes that are important in the context of hotel experience.
  • We compare the accuracy of ratings predicted by our technology against the ones provided by the user.
